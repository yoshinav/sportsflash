Using the Google Android platform, SportsFlash is a mobile application to create, manage, and participate in sports fantasy leagues from the convenience of your mobile device.  Socialize with your fantasy league community using social networking applications, and be connected to the dynamic flux of your fantasy league, 24/7, anywhere.
